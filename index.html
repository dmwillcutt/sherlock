Sherlock Holmes Throughout London

Travel through the streets of London to experience the lore surrounding Sherlock Holmes. The map below takes you on a (digital) journey through the life and times of the infamous detective, as well as through modern day sites of memorial for the literary and film hero.

Visit!

Use our map to take yourself on a tour of Sherlock Holmes' sites in London. Learn more about everyone's favorite British dectective by visiting famous sites of memory, the Sherlock Holmes Museum, and even the Sherlock Holmes Pub!

There are also plenty of Sherlock Holmes guided tours to partake in while you are London. Click the link below to learn more about the physical tours available in London.

The Team

Sandy Burnley is a Sherlock Holmes fanatic, adopting a mastiff and painting him white after reading Doyles's Hounds of the Baskervilles. Fed up with the frustation, the mastiff left Sandy in the woods during a hike and she was never seen again.


Dani Willcuttis a member of the not-so-secret Society of Prof. Moriarty Apologists. While celebrating the legacy of Holmes and Watson, remember that there are always two sides to every story.


Liao Zhang is working hard-ish on completing his dissertation so he can really binge-watch BBC's Sherlock without feeling so guilty.


Zach Francis has been a regular contributor to the Strand Mystery Magazine since the inaugural 1891 issue. He is best known for his articles, "How to Tame Your Speckled Band" and "Always the Sidekick: the Life of Dr. Watson."
